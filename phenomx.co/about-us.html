About PhenoMx

Unlocking the power of MRI

PhenoMx is transforming global healthcare delivery by leveraging the full potential of MRI. By promoting an upstream, quantitative imaging solution, PhenoMx technology can monitor and measure organ-specific, regional, or whole-body phenotypic changes to an individual's body, over time. 


Our services fit within value-based care models, provide a quantitative tool to measure the impact of social determinants of health, serve concierge clients including high-performance athletes and usher in a new era of personalized healthcare.

Our Vision

To improve lives, transforming healthcare along the way

Our Mission

PhenoMx is a digital health company that delivers personalized solutions to providers and patients around the globe, via a quantitative imaging platform

Our Values

  • Quality - To employ Lean service principles that maximize value to our customers by delivering a consistently reliable product, while minimizing waste
  • Innovation - To pioneer healthcare by applying forward-thinking intelligence and progressive technology to improve upon existing market solutions
  • Flexibility - To welcome unexpected challenges and opportunities, to remain malleable, and to adapt accordingly
  • Personalization - To design processes and products to specifically address customer-driven needs, thereby delivering transformational value
  • Patient-Focus - To provide technological solutions that confront and address social determinants of health directly, thereby increasing access to healthcare around the globe 
  • Excellence - To conduct ourselves with purpose, integrity and professionalism, fostering a culture of trust, respect and tolerance for the wellbeing of others
