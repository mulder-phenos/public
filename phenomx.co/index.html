The Digital Century

PhenoMx is commercializing a Personalized Digital Physical Examination to measure major vital organs & tissues of the whole body. Using non-invasive MRI scanning, we are pushing the boundaries of precision medicine, personalized wellness & human longevity.

Population Health

Our platform leverages existing MRI infrastructure to target Social Determinants of Health directly by providing upstream screening. Employing PhenoMx in population health directives encourages real-time imaging and tracking of pathology progression over time. 

Personalized Wellness

Our solution offers healthcare consumers, corporate wellness customers, high performance athletes and health-invested individuals the unique ability to measure and track personal wellness.
