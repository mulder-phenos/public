PHENOCHEST (TM): Rapid CT imaging protocol

Supporting Public Health Efforts to Address the COVID-19 Pandemic

In response to the ongoing global COVID-19 pandemic, PhenoMx has created the PhenoCHEST (TM) rapid CT screening protocol. This new tool leverages non-invasive CT scanning, digital chest X-Rays and start-of-the-art Artificial Intelligence algorithms to rapidly score findings and characteristics associated with the presence of COVID-19. PhenoCHEST (TM) has been developed using a limited real-world data set from patients infected with COVID-19 in both China and South Korea.

* PhenoCHEST is based on clinical images derived from equipment that has been cleared by respective regulatory authorities and government notified bodies. This tool is being provided to healthcare systems and clinicians on an investigational basis.
